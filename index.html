What makes Open CXP unique?

Open CXP database allows businesses to analyze and manage their financial and operational data in a structured and efficient manner.
Power of data-driven decision making

Open CXP is designed to handle complex and large-scale data, providing organizations with the ability to perform advanced analytics, forecasting, budgeting, and reporting. By centralizing and organizing data across various dimensions, Open CXP enables possibility to gain valuable insights into organization’s performance and make informed strategic decisions.

Commitment to open source principles

Our open source approach fosters collaboration and innovation within the CPM community. We actively encourage developers, data analysts, and business professionals to contribute to the development and improvement of Open CXP. This collaborative environment ensures that our solution remains cutting-edge, adaptable, and responsive to the evolving needs of businesses.

Integration possibilities

Open CXP is designed to integrate seamlessly with other components of CPM tools and systems. Whether it's financial planning, consolidation, or reporting software, our database can easily integrate with existing solutions to create a comprehensive and unified CPM platform.
